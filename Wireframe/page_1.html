Mind-Boggling Facts About Gender Inequality

1. Women make up 46% of the total U.S. labor force.


2. Women make only 77.5 cents for every dollar that men earn.


3. Women in professional specialty occupations were found to earn just 72.7% of what men in the same position earned.


4. Women may work longer to receive the promotions that provide access to higher pay.


5. Women business owners employ 35% more people than all the Fortune 500 companies combined.

"Five from "10 Surprising Statistics on Women in the Workplace." CollegeTimes. 2010. Web. 25 July 2016."
